About Space Waves

Space Waves is an intense space shooter game where you pilot a powerful starship through waves of alien enemies. Your mission is to defend the galaxy from the alien invasion while upgrading your ship and collecting valuable resources.

With its stunning visuals, engaging gameplay, and challenging difficulty progression, Space Waves offers an exciting gaming experience that will test your reflexes and strategic thinking. How long can you survive in the vastness of space?

Game Controls

Keyboard Controls

  • Arrow Keys: Move ship
  • Space: Fire weapons
  • Shift: Special ability

Touch Controls

  • Swipe: Move ship
  • Tap: Fire weapons
  • Double Tap: Special ability
