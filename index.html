About Traffic Rider VVQ

Traffic Rider VVQ is an intense endless motorcycle racing game where you navigate through heavy traffic at high speeds. Your goal is to survive as long as possible while performing stunts and collecting power-ups.

With its fast-paced gameplay, stunning visuals, and challenging difficulty progression, Traffic Rider VVQ offers an exciting gaming experience that will test your reflexes and timing. How far can you ride before crashing?

Game Controls

Keyboard Controls

  • Arrow Keys: Control motorcycle
  • Space: Perform stunts
  • Shift: Boost

Touch Controls

  • Swipe: Control direction
  • Tap: Perform stunts
  • Double Tap: Boost
