About the Game

Idle Breakout combines classic brick-breaking gameplay with addictive idle mechanics. Launch your ball to destroy bricks and earn coins, then use those coins to upgrade your paddle, unlock power-ups, and automate your progress. The game continues to earn coins even when you're offline, making it perfect for both active play and idle progression.

How to Play

Objective: Destroy all bricks to advance to the next level.
Controls: Use your mouse or touch to move the paddle and bounce the ball.
Strategy: Aim for clusters of bricks to maximize your score and coin earnings. Upgrade your paddle's size, ball speed, and special abilities to progress faster. Remember to prestige when progress slows to gain permanent bonuses!
